Our Mission

Purdue prides itself with holding some world records such as having the "World's Biggest Drum." We hope to continue on this journey by continuing to break even more engineering world records that could be recognized by Guinness Book of World Records, for example. We hope that with your help in designing, fabricating, and assembling, that we can not only break new records but also learn valuable skills in engineering and team collaboration. Our club is open to everyone, regardless of major or skill set, and we hope to see you there! Boiler up!
